Hello! We are Comics Cubed- a locally owned and operated comic book shop right here in the heart of Kokomo. Located downtown, our store has gained a reputation as a place to explore comics from all eras. In our store, there is no telling what you will find as our expansive inventory keeps growing. Visit us today!
Our owner, Shawn, briefly attended Indiana University Kokomo for a business management degree. However, he soon found his calling as a full-time manager of a previous comic book shop here in Kokomo in the late 1980s. Fast forward to today, and he is the owner of Comics Cubed; his own comic book store. Being involved since 1989, Shawn has gained a lot of respect from his peers and customers alike as he has consistently provided a unique place for anyone to buy, trade, and sell comic books.
